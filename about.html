Our Story
Hadithi Yetu

Redefining Beauty in Tanzania

Kubadilisha Urembo Tanzania

Salooni Verse is more than an app—it's a full-fledged beauty ecosystem transforming the traditional beauty experience.

Salooni Verse ni zaidi ya programu—ni mfumo kamili wa urembo unaobadilisha uzoefu wa kawaida wa urembo.

Salooni Verse is a revolutionary beauty marketplace designed for Tanzania, connecting clients with professional beauty service providers in a seamless, interactive, and highly personalized way.
Salooni Verse ni soko la urembo la kimapinduzi lililobuniwa kwa ajili ya Tanzania, likiunganisha wateja na watoa huduma wa urembo wa kikazi kwa njia laini, ya kuingiliana, na ya kibinafsi sana.

Unlike traditional salon apps, Salooni Verse provides a comprehensive platform where beauty professionals can showcase their work, sell products, offer tutorials, and connect directly with clients. We aim to democratize access to beauty services, giving street stylists and independent artists visibility while allowing clients to easily find, book, and pay for services in one place.

Tofauti na programu za kawaida za saluni, Salooni Verse hutoa jukwaa kamili ambapo wataalamu wa urembo wanaweza kuonyesha kazi zao, kuuza bidhaa, kutoa mafunzo, na kuunganisha moja kwa moja na wateja. Tunakusudia kuleta usawa katika upatikanaji wa huduma za urembo, kuwapa wakinyozi wa mitaani na wasanii huru umaarufu huku wakiwaruhusu wateja kupata, kufanya maagizo, na kulipa huduma kwa urahisi katika sehemu moja.
