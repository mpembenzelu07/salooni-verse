Frequently Asked Questions Maswali Yanayoulizwa Mara Kwa Mara

How do I book a service? Ninawezaje kuhifadhi huduma?

Simply browse categories, select a professional, choose your service, and book directly through our platform. You'll receive instant confirmation. Chaguo msingi vinjari aina, chagua mtaalamu, chagua huduma yako, na uhifadhi moja kwa moja kupitia jukwaa letu. Utapokea uthibitishaji wa papo hapo.

Are payments secure? Malipo yako salama?

Yes! We use bank-level encryption and secure payment gateways. Your financial information is never stored on our servers. Ndio! Tunatumia usimbaji wa kiwango cha benki na milango salama ya malipo. Taarifa zako za kifedha hazihifadhiwi kwenye seva zetu.

How do professionals join? Wataalamu wanawezaje kujiunga?

Beauty professionals can apply through our website or app. We verify credentials and provide training on using our platform effectively. Wataalamu wa urembo wanaweza kutumia maombi kupitia tovuti yetu au programu. Tunaidhinisha vyeti na kutoa mafunzo juu ya matumizi ya jukwaa letu kwa ufanisi.

What areas do you serve? Je, unahudumia maeneo gani?

Currently serving Dar es Salaam, Arusha, Mwanza, and Zanzibar. We're expanding to more cities across Tanzania soon. Kwa sasa tunahudumia Dar es Salaam, Arusha, Mwanza, na Zanzibar. Tunapanua kwa miji zaidi Tanzania hivi karibuni.
