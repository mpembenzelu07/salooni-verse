Frequently Asked Questions

Maswali Yanayoulizwa Mara kwa Mara

How do I book a beauty service?

Ninawezaje kufanya maagizo ya huduma ya urembo?

Simply download our app, browse through our premium categories, select your preferred professional, choose your service, and book your appointment. You can also book through our website.

Pakua programu yetu, vinjari kupitia aina zetu za hali ya juu, chagua mtaalamu unayempenda, chagua huduma yako, na fanya maagizo ya madi yako. Unaweza pia kufanya maagizo kupitia tovuti yetu.

How can I become a beauty professional on Salooni Verse?

Ninawezaje kuwa mtaalamu wa urembo kwenye Salooni Verse?

Visit our "Become a Pro" section on the app or website, complete the registration form with your credentials and portfolio, and our team will review your application within 48 hours.

Tembelea sehemu yetu ya "Kuwa Mtaalamu" kwenye programu au tovuti, kamilisha fomu ya usajili na sifa zako na potifolio yako, na timu yetu itakagua programu yako ndani ya masaa 48.

Are home services available in my area?

Huduma za nyumbani zinapatikana katika eneo langu?

Home services are available in major cities across Tanzania. When booking, the app will show you professionals available in your specific location.

Huduma za nyumbani zinapatikana katika miji mikuu kote Tanzania. Unapofanya maagizo, programu itakuonyesha wataalamu waliopo katika eneo lako maalum.
