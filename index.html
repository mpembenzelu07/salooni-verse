About Salooni Verse

Kuhusu Salooni Verse

Our Mission

Dhamira Yetu

Salooni Verse is a revolutionary beauty marketplace designed for Tanzania, connecting clients with professional beauty service providers, including salon hairstylists, street braiders, makeup artists, nail technicians, skincare specialists, and more.

Salooni Verse ni soko la urembo la kimapinduzi lililobuniwa kwa ajili ya Tanzania, likiunganisha wateja na watoa huduma wa urembo wa kikazi, pamoja na wakinyozi wa saluni, wapiga msokoto wa mitaani, wasanii wa make-up, wataalamu wa kucha, wataalamu wa utunzaji wa ngozi, na zaidi.

Unlike traditional salon apps, Salooni Verse provides a comprehensive platform where beauty professionals can showcase their work, sell products, offer tutorials, and connect directly with clients in a seamless, interactive, and highly personalized way.

Tofauti na programu za kawaida za saluni, Salooni Verse hutoa jukwaa kamili ambapo wataalamu wa urembo wanaweza kuonyesha kazi zao, kuuza bidhaa, kutoa mafunzo, na kuunganisha moja kwa moja na wateja kwa njia laini, ya kuingiliana, na ya kibinafsi sana.

Why We Stand Out

Kwa Nini Tunajitokeza

  • Inclusivity: Combines both salon and street-level professionals
  • Uwiano: Inachanganya wataalamu wa saluni na wa mitaani
  • Personalization: AI-driven recommendations and virtual try-ons
  • Ubinafsishaji: Mapendekezo yanayotokana na AI na kujaribu mtandaoni
  • Education & Skill Development: Tutorials and paid courses for growth
  • Elimu na Ukuzaji wa Ujuzi: Mafunzo na kozi za malipo kwa ajili ya ukuaji
  • Community & Engagement: Social features create loyalty and repeat use
  • Jamii na Ushiriki: Vipengele vya kijamii huunda uaminifu na matumizi ya mara kwa mara
